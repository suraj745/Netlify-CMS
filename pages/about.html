• About

  • Alok Ranjan Tiwari,

  • a dynamic corporate journalist and media entrepreneur, has carved a
    niche for himself in the world of journalism. His early decision to pursue
    this path at the age of 19 was a testament to his unwavering
    determination and passion for making a difference.


    Currently serving as the Editor-in-Chief of the prestigious National Hindi
    News Magazine 'Abhyuday Vatsalyam,' Alok is committed to delivering
    impactful content that resonates with readers nationwide. As the
    Executive Director of Eternal Corporate Media Pvt. Ltd, he blends
    innovation and creativity to create powerful media experiences that
    leave a lasting impression.
